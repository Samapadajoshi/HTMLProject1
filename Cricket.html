Cricket

About Cricket

Cricket is a team sport that involves batting, bowling, and fielding. It is played with a bat and ball on an oval-shaped field. The goal of the game is to score more runs than the other team.

How it's played

  • Two teams of 11 players compete
  • The bowler delivers the ball to the batsman at the other end of the pitch
  • The batsman tries to hit the ball and run to the other end of the pitch
  • The bowler tries to hit the wicket with the ball to dismiss the batsman
  • The team with the most runs at the end of the match wins

Where it's played

  1. Cricket originated in England
  2. It's also popular in countries that were once British colonies, such as India, Pakistan, Australia, South Africa, and the West Indies
  3. Cricket is played in more than 100 countries
